Our Story

FOTU was born from a simple question: What if fabric could think? What if the patterns that surround us could evolve, respond, and tell stories through their very structure?

We are a collective of digital artists, programmers, and textile enthusiasts who believe that the future of design lies at the intersection of traditional craftsmanship and computational creativity.

Each piece we create is a living system‚Äîpatterns that breathe, colors that shift with emotion, textures that respond to touch. We don't just make fabric; we cultivate digital ecosystems.
